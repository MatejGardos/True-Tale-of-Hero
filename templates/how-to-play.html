How to play?

Strike button - you will attack with your melee weapon dealing damage to the enemy but also taking damage
Shoot button - you will attack with your ranged weapon dealing damage to the enemy but not taking damage
Heal button - you will heal yourself by eating cheese - 20 health
Inventory button - you can see your equipment

Try to keep your health above 0, defeating enemies to progress through story (not implemented yet)

Hope you enjoy it!
